 # 模板preview

### **第一段：引入话题 + 明确观点 (Introduction)**

1. **引入话题：背景引入法 / 引用法 / 提问法**
   - "In recent years, [topic] has become a heated issue, attracting widespread attention from the public."
   - "As [topic] plays an increasingly significant role in [context, e.g., modern society/education/personal development], it has sparked heated discussions regarding [specific aspect]."
   - "Should [topic] be encouraged or discouraged? This question has triggered a heated debate among individuals from all walks of life."
2. **给出明确观点 (Thesis statement)**
   - "In my opinion, [your stance], as it [reason 1] and [reason 2]."
   - "From my perspective, [your stance], because [reason 1] and [reason 2], which will be elaborated in the following paragraphs."

------

### **第二段：展开论述 (Body Paragraph)**

1. **论据 1：支持观点的第一个理由**
   - **句型模板：**
     - "First and foremost, [reason 1]."
     - "To begin with, [reason 1], which is crucial for [specific benefit]."
     - "For example, [具体例子]. This example clearly illustrates that [解释例子如何支持理由]."
   - **衔接句：**
     - "Therefore, it is evident that [reason 1] is a vital factor in [supporting your stance]."
2. **论据 2：支持观点的第二个理由**
   - **句型模板：**
     - "In addition, [reason 2]."
     - "Moreover, [reason 2], which can further [具体作用或好处]."
     - "Take [another example] as an example. It demonstrates that [具体分析]."
   - **衔接句：**
     - "Consequently, this further proves that [reason 2] reinforces the importance of [your stance]."

------

### **第三段：总结观点 + 呼应主题 (Conclusion)**

1. **总结观点：重申立场和理由**
   - "In conclusion, [restate your stance] due to the reasons mentioned above, namely [reason 1] and [reason 2]."
   - "To sum up, I firmly believe that [your stance], as [reason 1] and [reason 2] both contribute significantly to [topic or related context]."
2. **展望未来 / 提供建议**
   - "It is high time that we took measures to [建议]. Only in this way can we [展望未来的目标]."
   - "In the future, more efforts should be made to [建议], so that [展望未来的目标]."
