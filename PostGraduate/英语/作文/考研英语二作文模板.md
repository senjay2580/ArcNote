## 一、大作文（Task B · 图表作文）

英语二大作文 2010 年起固定为**图表作文**，题型包括：
- 柱状图（bar chart） — 高频
- 饼状图（pie chart） — 高频
- 表格（table）
- 曲线图（graph）

**建议三段式结构**：数据描述段 → 原因分析段 → 评论总结段。

---

### 模板 1 · 柱状图 / 曲线图 / 表格 · 数据描述段
**来源：石雷鹏《30 个功能句》英二图表作文功能句 + 王江涛高分写作黄金模板**

> The chart above clearly **reflects the changes in / percentages of / information about** ___（描述对象）**between** ___ **and** ___（时间段）. It is noticeable that ___（项目 1）**increased / decreased considerably / slightly from** ___（起点数据）**in** ___ **to** ___（终点数据）**in** ___. Meanwhile, ___（项目 2）**was on a constant rise / decline from** ___ **to** ___ **over the same period**.

**解析：**
- `It is noticeable that` 是石雷鹏推荐的"趋势亮点"功能句，比 `We can see that` 更正式
- `considerably / slightly` 精准分级，避免只会用 `greatly`
- `constant rise / decline` 描述持续变化，适合曲线图
- `over the same period` 收尾统一两组数据的时间范围

---

### 模板 2 · 饼状图 · 数据描述段
**来源：王江涛《考研英语高分写作》图表作文模板**

> As is vividly depicted in the pie chart above, striking differences can be easily identified among the percentages of ___（描述对象）**in** ___（时间 / 地点）. To be specific, ___（最大项）**accounts for the lion's share, reaching** ___%, **followed by** ___ **at** ___%, **whereas** ___ **merely takes up** ___%.

**解析：**
- `accounts for the lion's share` 来自王江涛高频表达，替代 `takes up the largest part`
- `followed by … at …%, whereas …` 一句串联三项数据，结构紧凑
- `merely` 一词体现对比反差

---

### 模板 3 · 原因分析段（通用）
**来源：石雷鹏《30 个功能句》原因段功能句**

> **A number of factors could contribute to / account for** the phenomenon described above, **among which the following are the most conspicuous**. **In the first place**, ___（原因一，通常写经济或社会背景）. **Furthermore**, **with the rapid development of** ___, **an increasing number of people have come to realize that** ___（原因二，写观念转变）. **Last but not least**, ___（原因三，写政策 / 科技 / 教育）.

**解析：**
- `contribute to / account for` 是石雷鹏原因段首句双选结构
- `among which the following are the most conspicuous` 定语从句提升档次
- 三段式 `In the first place / Furthermore / Last but not least` 逻辑清晰、卷面整洁

---

### 模板 4 · 评论总结段（预测 / 建议 / 利弊三选一）
**来源：石雷鹏《30 个功能句》评论段功能句 + 真题范文汇总**

**（a）做预测 · 适用积极趋势**
> **In view of the analysis above**, it can be safely predicted that **the favorable trend will continue in the foreseeable future**, **provided that** ___（必要条件）.

**（b）写利弊 · 适用中性话题**
> **To sum up, this development is more like a double-edged sword**. **On the one hand**, ___（积极面）. **On the other hand**, ___（消极面）. **Therefore, what we should do is to maximize its merits while minimizing its demerits**.

**（c）提建议 · 适用消极趋势**
> **From my perspective**, **it is high time that effective measures were taken to reverse the current situation**. **For one thing**, the government **is supposed to** ___. **For another**, **individuals ought to** ___. **Only in this way can we** ___.

**解析：**
- 三段式结尾可根据题目话题自由切换
- `it is high time that + 虚拟语气（过去式）` 是王江涛反复强调的"加分句型"
- `Only in this way can we …` 部分倒装，卷面立刻加分

## 二、小作文（Task A · 应用文）

英语二小作文为 **100 词左右的书信 / 通知**，2010-2024 已考题型：建议信、推荐信、邀请信、道歉信、感谢信、祝贺信、投诉信、求职信、通知等。

**统一三段式**：写信目的 → 具体内容 → 期待回复。

---

### 模板 5 · 通用书信框架
**来源：石雷鹏《30 个功能句》小作文 14 个功能句**

**开头段（1 句）：**
> **I am** ___（身份，如 Li Ming, a student from Peking University）. **I am writing this letter to** ___（写信目的，如 extend my sincere gratitude to you for …）.

**主体段（2–3 句）：**
> **To begin with**, ___（要点一）. **In addition**, ___（要点二）. **Last but not least**, ___（要点三）.

**结尾段（1 句）：**
> **I would be highly grateful if you could take my suggestions / invitation / apology into account**. **I am looking forward to your early reply**.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

---

### 模板 6 · 建议信（Suggestion Letter）
**来源：参考 2017 年英语二 Task A 真题范文（建议图书馆改进服务）**

> Dear ___,
>
> **I am a loyal reader of your library**, **and I am writing this letter to put forward some suggestions concerning** ___.
>
> **To begin with**, **it would be better if** ___（建议一，用虚拟语气）. **Additionally**, **I would like to suggest that** ___ **should be** ___（建议二）. **Last but not least**, ___（建议三）.
>
> **I sincerely hope you will take my suggestions into serious consideration**. **Thank you for your time and patience**.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**解析：**
- `put forward some suggestions concerning …` 比 `give you some advice` 更书面
- 建议信核心句 `it would be better if + 虚拟语气` 是石雷鹏功能句 11

---

### 模板 7 · 推荐信（Recommendation Letter）
**来源：参考 2016 年英语二 Task A 真题范文（向外国朋友推荐中国电影）**

> Dear Jack,
>
> **Knowing that you are fond of** ___, **I am writing to recommend** ___ **to you**.
>
> **The reasons for my recommendation are as follows**. **For one thing**, ___（推荐理由一，如经典、口碑）. **For another**, ___（推荐理由二，如文化、趣味）. **Most importantly**, **I am sure you will** ___（预期收获）.
>
> **I would be much delighted if my recommendation could be of some help to you**. **Looking forward to your reply**.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**解析：**
- 开头 `Knowing that you are fond of …` 一句交代背景 + 动机，避免老套的 `How is everything going?`
- `would be much delighted if …` 是王江涛推荐的"委婉客气"结尾

---

### 模板 8 · 道歉信（Apology Letter）
**来源：参考 2018 年英语二 Task A 真题范文（向 Professor Smith 道歉缺课）**

> Dear ___,
>
> **I am writing this letter to express my sincere apology for** ___（道歉事由）, **and I sincerely hope you could accept my apology and forgive me for my carelessness**.
>
> **The reason for** ___ **is that** ___（解释客观原因）. **To make up for my fault**, **I would like to** ___（补救措施）. **If it is convenient for you**, **I wonder if we could** ___（具体提议）.
>
> **Once again**, **I am terribly sorry for any inconvenience I have caused**. **I am looking forward to your kind reply**.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**解析：**
- 道歉信"三步走"：道歉 → 解释 → 补救，严格对应石雷鹏小作文功能句 8–10
- `To make up for my fault` 提出补救是评分核心得分点

---

### 模板 9 · 邀请信（Invitation Letter）
**来源：历年英语二真题范文归纳**

> Dear ___,
>
> **On behalf of** ___, **I am writing to extend my sincere invitation to you for** ___（活动名称）, **which will be held in** ___ **on** ___（时间地点）.
>
> **The reasons why I sincerely invite you are as follows**. **Firstly**, ___（活动亮点）. **Secondly**, ___（对受邀人的意义）. **During the activity, you will have the opportunity to** ___.
>
> **It would be a great honor if you could come**. **I am looking forward to your early reply**.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

---

### 模板 10 · 感谢信（Thank-You Letter）
**来源：参考 2013 年英语二 Task A 真题范文（感谢教授帮助）**

> Dear ___,
>
> **I am writing this letter to express my heartfelt gratitude to you for** ___（感谢事由）.
>
> **Were it not for your generous help**, **I could never have** ___（假设不帮助的后果）. **Your kindness and help have meant a lot to me**, **and I will always cherish** ___.
>
> **Words fail me when I want to express my sincere appreciation**. **Please allow me to thank you again**, **and I sincerely hope that** ___（回报 / 祝福）.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**解析：**
- `Were it not for …` 倒装虚拟语气，石雷鹏点名的"感谢信神句"
- `Words fail me when …` 情感渲染句，替代俗套的 `Thank you very much`

## 四、小作文补全模板（覆盖全部英语二题型）

### 模板 11 · 投诉信（Complaint Letter）
**来源：参考 2019 年英语二 Task A 真题范文（投诉所购商品质量问题）**

> Dear Sir or Madam,
>
> **I am writing to lodge a complaint about** ___（投诉对象）**which I bought / received from your company on** ___（时间）.
>
> **The reasons for my dissatisfaction are as follows**. **To begin with**, ___（问题一：质量/服务瑕疵）. **What's worse**, ___（问题二：后果 / 影响）. **I must say that I am quite disappointed with** ___.
>
> **I would appreciate it if you could** ___（期望解决方案，如 refund / replacement / apology）**as soon as possible**. **Otherwise, I will have to resort to further action**.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**解析：**
- `lodge a complaint about` 是石雷鹏点名的投诉信标志性词组，比 `complain about` 正式
- `What's worse` 递进，展示逻辑层次
- `I would appreciate it if you could …` 礼貌但有力，比 `please help me` 得分高

---

### 模板 12 · 求职信（Application Letter）
**来源：参考 2014 年英语二 Task A 真题范文（申请志愿者岗位）**

> Dear Sir or Madam,
>
> **I am Li Ming, a senior student from Peking University**, **and I am writing to apply for the position of** ___（岗位名称）**advertised on** ___（信息来源）.
>
> **I venture to believe that I am qualified for this position for the following reasons**. **First**, **I have majored in** ___ **for** ___ **years, which has equipped me with** ___（专业能力）. **Second**, **I am proficient in** ___（技能）**and have previously worked as** ___（相关经历）. **Moreover**, **I am an enthusiastic team player**.
>
> **I would be most grateful if you could grant me an interview at your convenience**. **My résumé is enclosed for your reference**.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**解析：**
- `I venture to believe that I am qualified` 自信而不傲慢，王江涛高分句
- "教育背景 + 技能 + 性格"三点式最稳妥
- 结尾 `grant me an interview` 主动推进，评分亮点

---

### 模板 13 · 祝贺信（Congratulation Letter）
**来源：参考历年英语二真题范文**

> Dear ___,
>
> **I am writing to extend my warmest congratulations on** ___（祝贺事由，如 your being admitted to Tsinghua University）.
>
> **The news that** ___ **really delighted me**, **and I firmly believe that this achievement is nothing but the natural result of your** ___（天赋/努力）. **I can still remember how hard you worked when** ___（回忆过往努力细节）. **You truly deserve such honor**.
>
> **Once again, please accept my sincere congratulations**. **Wishing you even greater success in the days to come**.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**解析：**
- `extend my warmest congratulations on` 替代 `Congratulations!`，书面感强
- `nothing but the natural result of …` 既赞美又含蓄，避免谄媚
- 插入回忆细节 → 增加"个性化"，打破千篇一律模板感

---

### 模板 14 · 询问信（Inquiry Letter）
**来源：参考 2022 年英语二 Task A 真题范文（咨询暑期课程）**

> Dear Sir or Madam,
>
> **I am Li Ming, a sophomore from Peking University**. **I am writing to inquire about some detailed information concerning** ___（咨询对象，如 the summer program hosted by your institute）.
>
> **I would be grateful if you could answer several of my questions**. **First and foremost**, ___（问题一：时间 / 地点 / 费用）. **Besides**, ___（问题二：具体内容 / 资格要求）. **Last but not least**, ___（问题三：报名方式 / 截止日期）.
>
> **Thank you for your time and consideration**. **I am looking forward to your prompt reply**.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**解析：**
- `inquire about … concerning …` 双重限定，比 `ask about` 正式
- 三问点 "when/where/how" 结构最清晰
- `prompt reply` 暗示紧迫感

### 模板 15 · 介绍信 / 自我介绍（Introduction Letter）
**来源：参考 2011 年英语二 Task A 真题范文（向网友介绍自己）**

> Dear ___,
>
> **I am greatly honored to introduce myself to you as** ___（身份，如 your future roommate）. **My name is Li Ming**, **and I come from** ___.
>
> **As for my hobbies**, **I am particularly interested in** ___（爱好一）**and** ___（爱好二）. **In terms of my personality**, **I am an outgoing person who enjoys** ___. **Besides, I am willing to** ___（对对方表达诚意/合作意愿）.
>
> **I am eagerly looking forward to meeting you in person**. **If there is anything you want to know about me, please feel free to let me know**.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

### 模板 16 · 通知 / 告示（Notice / Announcement）
**来源：参考 2012 年英语二 Task A 真题范文（学生会通知招聘志愿者）**

> **NOTICE**
> **（顶格、居中、大写）**
> December 23rd, 2025
>
> **In order to** ___（活动目的，如 enrich students' extracurricular life）, **the Students' Union is going to hold** ___（活动名称）**on** ___（时间）**at** ___（地点）.
>
> **All those who are interested in** ___ **are warmly welcomed to participate**. **The activity will mainly include** ___（内容一）**and** ___（内容二）. **Those who would like to join us are required to sign up at** ___ **before** ___（截止日期）. **For further information, please feel free to contact Li Ming at** ___（联系方式）.
>
> **Come and join us! Your participation will be highly appreciated**.
>
> The Students' Union

**解析：**
- 通知不写 Dear / Yours sincerely，**顶端写"NOTICE"+ 日期**，底端写发布单位
- `In order to … is going to hold …` 是通知标志性句式
- 五要素：**目的 / 时间 / 地点 / 内容 / 报名方式** 缺一不可

## 五、大作文补全模板（按图表类型独立）

### 模板 17 · 曲线图（Line Graph）· 数据描述段
**来源：参考 2010 / 2014 / 2020 年英语二大作文真题结构**

> **What is clearly presented in the line graph above is** ___（主题）**from** ___ **to** ___（时间段）. **According to the data**, ___（对象一）**witnessed a steady upward trend, climbing from** ___ **to** ___, **with an increase of approximately** ___%. **By contrast**, ___（对象二）**experienced a sharp decline, dropping from** ___ **to** ___ **over the same period**.

**解析：**
- `witnessed / experienced` 拟人手法替代 `there was`，王江涛高分技巧
- `upward trend / sharp decline` 是曲线图专用术语
- `approximately` 避免精确数字背错被扣分

---

### 模板 18 · 表格（Table）· 数据描述段
**来源：参考 2011 / 2016 年英语二大作文真题**

> **The table above presents a comparison of** ___（对比对象）**in** ___（时间 / 地点）. **A careful analysis of the figures reveals that** ___（最大项）**occupies the first place with** ___%（or the figure of ___）, **significantly higher than** ___（对象二）**which accounts for only** ___%. **It is also worth mentioning that** ___（对象三）**shows a unique pattern of** ___（独特特征）.

**解析：**
- 表格作文核心：**比较 + 排名**，必须指出"第一 / 第二 / 特殊项"
- `A careful analysis of the figures reveals that` 替代 `we can see that`
- `It is also worth mentioning that` 引出次要数据，体现层次

---

### 模板 19 · 柱状图（Bar Chart）· 数据描述段独立版
**来源：参考 2013 / 2017 / 2022 / 2023 年英语二大作文真题**

> **As can be clearly seen from the bar chart above**, **there exists remarkable disparity in** ___（对比对象）**among** ___（比较类别）. **Specifically**, ___（最高项）**stands out with a figure of** ___, **which is nearly** ___ **times as much as that of** ___（最低项）. **Meanwhile**, ___（中间项）**takes a middle position at** ___.

**解析：**
- `there exists remarkable disparity in` 是柱状图"对比"专属表达
- `X times as much as` 倍数比较是高分句型，必考
- "最高 / 中间 / 最低"三分法是柱状图最保险的描述顺序

## 七、书信格式细则（避免扣格式分）

> **格式失分是最冤的**：内容写得再好，格式错了直接扣 2–3 分。英语二小作文满分 10 分，扣 3 分直接掉档。

### 格式 1 · 传统书信（2010–2017 主流）
```
                                         Dear Sir or Madam,     ← 顶格，逗号，空一行

    I am writing …       ← 每段首行空 4 字母（或 1 个 Tab）

    To begin with …

    I am looking forward …

                                         Yours sincerely,      ← 空一行，右对齐或靠左
                                         Li Ming               ← 另起一行
```

**注意事项：**
- **不写日期、不写寄信人地址**（考研小作文不需要）
- `Dear` 后面接 `Sir or Madam,` / `Mr. Smith,` / `David,` — **必须加逗号**
- 结尾套语可选：`Yours sincerely,` / `Yours faithfully,` / `Best regards,`
- 署名 **Li Ming**（李明），**严禁写自己真实姓名**，否则按 0 分处理

---

### 格式 2 · 电子邮件（2018–2025 主流）
```
Dear John,                ← 与传统书信相同

    I am writing to …    ← 首行缩进

    …

    Looking forward to your early reply.

Yours,                    ← 邮件体套语可更随意：Yours, / Best, / Cheers,
Li Ming
```

**邮件体与传统书信的区别：**
| 项目 | 传统书信 | 邮件（2018+） |
|---|---|---|
| 称呼 | Dear Sir or Madam, | Dear + 具体名字（题目已给）|
| 正式度 | 正式 | 半正式 |
| 结尾 | Yours sincerely, | Yours, / Best regards, |
| 段落 | 首行缩进 | 缩进 or 顶格都行，统一即可 |

**陷阱提醒**：虽然是邮件体，**不要写 Subject / To / From 等邮件头**，考研不需要。

---

### 格式 3 · 通知 / 告示（2015 考过）
```
                          NOTICE              ← 顶端居中，全大写，可加粗
                       December 23rd, 2025    ← 第二行写日期（可选）

    In order to … the Students' Union is going to hold …（正文顶格或缩进皆可）
    
    …

                                          The Students' Union  ← 结尾右对齐写发布单位
```

**通知的核心格式规则：**
- **无 Dear、无 Yours sincerely**
- 顶端居中写 `NOTICE`（或 `ANNOUNCEMENT`）
- 结尾写发布单位（如 `The Students' Union` / `The Postgraduates' Association`），**不写 Li Ming**
- 正文必须包含五要素：**目的 / 时间 / 地点 / 内容 / 报名方式**

---

### 格式 4 · 备忘录（Memo，少考但可能出现）
```
MEMO
To: All Staff
From: Li Ming, General Manager
Date: December 23rd, 2025
Subject: Training Session Arrangement

正文（顶格，不缩进）
```

**此格式英语二历年未考过**，但大纲涵盖，建议了解即可。

---

### 常见格式错误清单 ❌

| 错误 | 后果 |
|---|---|
| 署名写自己真实姓名 | 按 0 分或严重扣分 |
| 通知写 Dear / Yours | 格式错，扣 2 分 |
| `Dear Sir or Madam` 后忘记加逗号 | 标点扣分 |
| 段落不分，一段到底 | 结构分降档 |
| 邮件写 Subject / To / From 头 | 多余，虽不扣但显示格式不熟 |
| 日期写成 `2025/12/23` | 英文应为 `December 23rd, 2025` 或 `23 December 2025` |
| 全文大写或全文小写 | 基本格式失守，直接降档 |

## 八、小作文补全模板 · 近年高频题型

### 模板 20 · 接受邀请信（Acceptance Letter · 2017 真题同款）
**来源：参考 2017 年英语二 Task A 真题范文（接受 Williams 教授演讲邀请）**

> Dear Prof. Williams,
>
> **I am writing to express my sincere appreciation for your kind invitation to deliver a speech on** ___（演讲主题）**to your international students**. **It is my great honor to accept this invitation**.
>
> **Regarding the main points of the speech**, **I would like to focus on the following aspects**. **Firstly**, ___（要点一：核心介绍）. **Secondly**, ___（要点二：具体展开）. **In addition**, **I plan to share some** ___（个人素材，如 stories / pictures / anecdotes）**to make the speech more vivid and engaging**.
>
> **If you have any other requirements or suggestions, please feel free to let me know**. **I am looking forward to meeting you and your students soon**.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**解析：**
- 接受邀请信 = 感谢 + 明确接受 + 具体计划 三段式
- `It is my great honor to accept` 是 2017 范文指定表达
- 主体必须写出"演讲/参与的具体要点"，否则等于废话

---

### 模板 21 · 复合功能信（感谢 + 建议 / 祝贺 + 建议 · 2011、2016 真题同款）
**来源：2011 / 2016 英语二 Task A 真题范文归纳**

> Dear ___,
>
> **I am writing to extend my heartfelt gratitude / congratulations on** ___（感谢/祝贺事由）. **Meanwhile**, **in response to your request for advice**, **I would like to share some of my thoughts with you**.
>
> **First and foremost**, ___（建议一：最核心的一条）. **Furthermore**, **it would be wise to** ___（建议二：具体做法）. **Last but not least**, ___（建议三：长远心态/习惯）.
>
> **Hopefully my suggestions will be of some help to you**. **Wishing you all the best in** ___（祝愿对方下一阶段的事）.
>
> Yours sincerely,
> Li Ming

**解析：**
- 复合功能信的关键是 **"第一段执行功能 A + 转折 Meanwhile/Also + 第二段执行功能 B"**
- 英语二近 16 年考过 **5 次复合题**（2010、2011、2014、2016、2020），占比约 31%，说明复合类型必须单独训练
- 转折连接词 `Meanwhile / In addition / On top of that` 一句桥接两段功能

## 七、反模式 vs 正确模式（高分铁律）

> **判卷现实**：英语二作文平均批阅时间 **30–60 秒**，评分流程是"**印象分 → 找亮点 → 定档**"。
> 阅卷老师一天批 80+ 份，**烂大街模板一眼识破**，直接归入 9–11 分档。
> 本节记录"**一写就扣分**"的反模式和"**一用就加分**"的正确模式，每次下笔前必看。

---

### 反模式 A：共享模板原封照抄 ❌

| 反模式（几十万人都在写） | 问题 | 正确模式（改写变体） |
|---|---|---|
| `As is vividly depicted in the picture/chart above …` | 烂大街，出现频率 >60% | `What is clearly presented in the bar chart above is …` / `The bar chart above provides an illustration of …` |
| `It is universally acknowledged that …` | 被阅卷老师拉入黑名单 | `In recent years, X has sparked heated discussions among the general public` / `There is no denying that X is gaining unprecedented attention` |
| `With the rapid development of economy/society …` | 套在任何题目上都像废话 | `Against the backdrop of + 具体话题（the booming e-commerce / the aging population）` |
| `Every coin has two sides` | 小学生级别表达 | `This development is more like a double-edged sword` |
| `In a word / All in all` | 过于口语 | `In view of the analysis above` / `Taking all these factors into consideration` |

**铁律**：**背 1 句 → 准备 3 个改写变体**，考场上挑最贴合题目的那一句写。

---

### 反模式 B：审题不准、图表类型错配 ❌

| 反模式 | 问题 | 正确模式 |
|---|---|---|
| 饼状图题写 `increased from … to …` | 饼状图是**静态占比**，没有时间变化 | 饼状图只写 `accounts for / takes up / makes up` |
| 柱状图题写 `on a constant rise` | 柱状图常是**分类对比**，不是趋势 | 柱状图用 `there exists remarkable disparity among …` |
| 曲线图题只写最大值和最小值 | 曲线图核心是**趋势**而非极值 | 曲线图必须写 `trend / fluctuation / peak at … / bottom out at …` |
| 表格题把数据全部罗列 | 100 字写不完，也看不出重点 | 表格只写 **"第一 + 最后 + 特殊项"** 三项 |
| 不看单位（%、万、亿）就抄数字 | 数据错扣大分 | 先圈出单位再写 `approximately / roughly / nearly` 避免精确背错 |

**铁律**：**先判断图表类型 → 再选对应模板句式**，绝不能用柱状图模板套饼状图。

---

### 反模式 C：生僻词硬塞、搭配错误 ❌

| 反模式 | 问题 | 正确模式 |
|---|---|---|
| `The phenomenon is ameliorating nowadays` | `ameliorate` 是不及物动词，中式搭配 | 直接用 `is improving` 或 `is getting better` |
| `paramount / exacerbate / unprecedented` 堆砌 | 前后搭配错，阅卷人看出是硬背 | 1 段 1 个高级词就够，**准确 > 高级** |
| `I think / I think / I think` 重复 | 词汇单调 | 轮换 `I deem / I maintain / I hold the view that / From my perspective` |
| `very important / very good / very bad` | 小学词汇 | `crucial / vital / indispensable` / `of great significance` |
| `many people / a lot of people` | 表达贫乏 | `a substantial number of people / a considerable proportion of the public` |

**铁律**：**写作词汇 ≤ 2000 个足够，搭配准才是关键**。遇到不确定搭配，宁可用简单词。

---

### 反模式 D：逻辑混乱、内容空洞 ❌

| 反模式 | 问题 | 正确模式 |
|---|---|---|
| 原因段全是 `with the development of society` | 空话 | 扣题具体化：教育类→`intensified competition in the job market`；科技类→`the booming of mobile internet` |
| 结尾只写 `So, we should do something` | 无行动方案 | `For one thing, the government is supposed to … For another, individuals ought to …` |
| 三段之间无逻辑词 | 段落割裂 | 段首必放 `To begin with / Furthermore / In view of the above` |
| 同一话题反复说 `it is important` | 原地绕圈 | 用 **"现象 → 原因 → 建议"** 三层递进，不回头 |
| 举例用 "我朋友 / 我同学" | 太私人，不权威 | 用通用例子：中国高铁 / 移动支付 / 共享单车 / 5G 普及 |

**铁律**：**每段一个核心句 + 2 个支撑句**，绝不超过 3 句话展开一个点。

---

### 反模式 E：格式与卷面 ❌

| 反模式 | 问题 | 正确模式 |
|---|---|---|
| 小作文写 `Dear Sir`, 下一行直接正文 | 格式分丢 | `Dear Sir or Madam,` 空一行再写正文；结尾空一行后 `Yours sincerely,` 另起一行写 `Li Ming` |
| 通知首行写 `Dear all` | 通知无收件人 | 通知顶端居中大写 `NOTICE`，底端写发布单位 |
| 全文一段到底 | 扣结构分 | 大作文 **3 段**、小作文 **3 段**，段首缩进 4 字母 |
| 反复涂改、箭头插句 | 卷面脏→印象分直接降档 | 写前打 30 秒腹稿；宁可少写也不乱改 |
| 写满超过 200 字（大作文） | 超纲不加分反扣时间 | 大作文 **150–180 字**，小作文 **100 字左右**，严格控字 |

**铁律**：**卷面干净 = 第一印象分**，印象分是档位的决定因素。

---

### ✅ 正确模式总则（高分 4 条铁律）

1. **骨架通用，血肉定制**：三段结构可以死背，但句子必须换过词和搭配
2. **1 段 1 个亮点就够**：每段留 1 句自己背熟的独家句（倒装/虚拟/定从），其他句子求稳
3. **准确 > 高级**：用错的难词不如用对的简单词，**零语法错误**本身就是 14 分起评
4. **扣题 > 扣模板**：写前先在卷面空白处标出"**题目关键词 3 个**"，每段至少出现 1 个关键词

## 十一、模板化破局指南 · 阅卷人视角逆向破解

> **核心认知颠覆**：阅卷老师**根本不反模板**。
> 研招网 / 考研帮参加过阅卷的老师一致口径：
> **"高分还是低分的主要因素，不是是否使用了模板，而是语言质量是否高级，是否出现特别低级的错误。"**
>
> 评分公式 = **错误率低（下限）× 闪光点多（上限）**

---

### 11.1 模板作文低分的 5 大真凶

| 真凶 | 典型症状 | 破解方案 |
|---|---|---|
| ① 填空时的低级错误 | `I am looked forward to hear from you`（两个语法错） | 每个动词的时态/语态/非谓语形式背死，**宁可少写漂亮话也别错 -ing** |
| ② 模板类型错配 | 图表作文用"对比正反"模板；对朋友说 `the government should` | 每题型**专用模板**，禁止一套打天下 |
| ③ 关键词与主题零关联 | `heated topic` 套"大学生劳动课收获" | **开头第一句必须出现题目原词或近义替换** |
| ④ 关键词反复 / 词汇单一 | 整篇 `many / good / think / show` | 准备 **10 组高频同义替换**（见 §11.4） |
| ⑤ 卷面 + 字数失控 | <150 或 >220；涂改 5 处以上；一段到底 | 考前**掐表手写 3 篇全真**，固化字数和卷面 |

### 11.2 阅卷人的"闪光点雷达" · 5 类亮点句型

> **规则**：每篇用 2–3 个，全用就刻意，不用就平庸。

#### ① 倒装句（每篇必放 1 个）
- `Only by working together can we build a better future.`（部分倒装）
- `Never before has China witnessed such rapid development.`（否定前置倒装）
- `Not until we take action will the situation improve.`

#### ② 虚拟语气（每篇必放 1 个）
- `It is high time that effective measures were taken.`
- `Were it not for your generous help, I could never have succeeded.`
- `If I were in your position, I would definitely choose …`

#### ③ 非谓语短语（开头 / 过渡用）
- `Confronted with this growing trend, we must take immediate action.`
- `Taking all these factors into consideration, I firmly believe that …`
- `Having witnessed the significant changes, I can't help wondering …`

#### ④ 强调句型
- `It is the timely intervention of the government that has changed the situation.`
- `What we need most is not complaint but concrete action.`

#### ⑤ 定语从句（加深结构层次）
- `There exist numerous factors, among which the most conspicuous is …`
- `This is a phenomenon that deserves our serious attention.`

### 范文 1 · 大作文（柱状图 · 某高校学生课余阅读方式变化）

**题目情境**：某调查显示 2018–2024 年间，某高校学生数字阅读与纸质阅读比例变化（柱状图）。
（说明：本题目融合 2019、2020、2024 英语二真题结构，覆盖柱状图描述 + 原因 + 评论，**题目一换即可套用到任何"某趋势变化"型柱状图**。）

#### 英文范文（约 198 词）

> **§1 · 数据描述**
>
> What is clearly presented in the bar chart above is the changing pattern of college students' leisure reading habits from 2018 to 2024. As can be readily observed, the proportion of students engaged in digital reading witnessed a steady upward trend, climbing from approximately 30% to over 65%. By contrast, those who preferred traditional paper reading experienced a noticeable decline, dropping from nearly 55% to about 25% over the same period.
>
> **§2 · 原因分析**
>
> A multitude of factors could account for this phenomenon, among which the following are the most conspicuous. First and foremost, with the rapid advance of digital transformation, mobile devices have become indispensable companions, enabling students to access vast reading resources anytime and anywhere. Furthermore, the fast-paced campus lifestyle has made fragmented reading on screens a more practical choice than lingering over heavy volumes. Last but not least, the rising awareness of sustainable development has prompted many to embrace paperless reading as an eco-friendly alternative.
>
> **§3 · 评论升华**
>
> Taking all these factors into consideration, it is high time that we took a balanced view of this transition. Only by combining the convenience of digital reading with the depth of traditional reading can we truly cultivate lifelong learning habits. Personally, I firmly believe that this trend, though seemingly inevitable, deserves our thoughtful reflection rather than blind embrace.

#### 中文参考译文

> 上方柱状图清晰呈现了 2018 至 2024 年间某高校学生课余阅读习惯的变化。可以明显观察到，选择数字阅读的学生比例呈稳步上升趋势，从约 30% 攀升至超过 65%。相比之下，偏好传统纸质阅读的学生则经历了显著下降，在同一时期从近 55% 跌至约 25%。
>
> 诸多因素可以解释这一现象，其中最为突出的有以下几点。首先，随着数字化转型的迅猛推进，移动设备已成为不可或缺的伙伴，让学生可以随时随地获取海量阅读资源。其次，快节奏的校园生活使得屏幕碎片化阅读比捧读厚重书本更为实用。最后，对可持续发展的日益关注促使许多人将无纸化阅读视为一种更环保的选择。
>
> 综合这些因素，我们是时候以平衡视角看待这一转变了。唯有将数字阅读的便捷与传统阅读的深度相结合，我们才能真正培养起终身学习的习惯。就我个人而言，我坚信这一趋势尽管看似不可避免，但仍值得我们深思而非盲从。

#### 亮点标注（逐句拆解 · 为什么能拿高分）

| # | 原句 | 加分点 |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | `What is clearly presented in the bar chart above is ___` | **主语从句做开头**，比 `As is vividly depicted` 新颖 |
| 2 | `witnessed a steady upward trend, climbing from ___ to ___` | **拟人 + 非谓语现在分词**伴随状语 |
| 3 | `By contrast, those who preferred ___ experienced a noticeable decline` | **对比连接词 + 定语从句 + 拟人动词** |
| 4 | `A multitude of factors could account for this phenomenon, **among which** the following are the most conspicuous` | **定语从句（非限定）**，石雷鹏原因段黄金句 |
| 5 | `with the rapid advance of digital transformation` | **情境词组（科技主题）**，替代烂大街 `with the development of society` |
| 6 | `indispensable companions` | **同义替换**（indispensable = important）|
| 7 | `lingering over heavy volumes` | **动名词 + 具象动词**，比 `reading books` 高级 |
| 8 | `sustainable development` + `eco-friendly` | **情境词组（环保主题）** |

### 范文 2 · 小作文（建议信 · 邮件体）

**题目情境**：朋友 David 周日想在艺术展和机器人展中选一个去，请你回信给出建议和理由。
（说明：本题为 **2023 年英语二 Task A 真题同款**，**一换主题即可套用到任何"二选一"建议信**。）

#### 英文范文（约 112 词）

> Dear David,
>
> **§1 · 写信目的**
>
> Having learned that you are torn between the art exhibition and the robot show this Sunday, I am writing to offer my honest suggestion and share my reasons with you.
>
> **§2 · 具体建议 + 理由**
>
> From my perspective, the robot show certainly deserves priority. To begin with, it showcases cutting-edge achievements in digital transformation and artificial intelligence, which are reshaping our future at an unprecedented pace. Furthermore, its interactive sessions allow visitors to experience the technology firsthand, offering not merely knowledge but genuine inspiration. Last but not least, given your enthusiasm for critical thinking and innovation, such an event will undoubtedly spark your intellectual curiosity.
>
> **§3 · 期待回复**
>
> Should you find my suggestion appealing, I would be more than happy to accompany you. Looking forward to your reply.
>
> Yours,
> Li Ming

#### 中文参考译文

> 亲爱的 David：
>
> 得知你正在艺术展和机器人展之间犹豫不决，我写信给你一些真诚的建议并分享我的理由。
>
> 在我看来，机器人展无疑更值得你优先选择。首先，它展示了数字化转型和人工智能领域的前沿成就，这些技术正以前所未有的速度重塑我们的未来。其次，其互动环节可以让参观者亲身体验科技，带来的不仅是知识，更是真正的灵感。最后，鉴于你对批判性思维和创新的热情，这样的活动必然会激发你的求知欲。
>
> 如果你觉得我的建议可取，我非常愿意陪你一同前往。期待你的回复。
>
> 你的，
> 李明

| # | 原句 | 加分点 |

| 6 | `offering not merely knowledge but genuine inspiration` | **非谓语 + not merely ... but ...** 递进结构 |

| 8 | `will undoubtedly spark your intellectual curiosity` | **情境词组（教育主题）** + undoubtedly 副词加强 |

| 10 | `I would be more than happy to accompany you` | **虚拟语气 + more than happy** 委婉热情 |

**覆盖的主题情境词**：科技（2 个）、教育（2 个）
**覆盖的书信格式**：✅ Dear + 逗号 ✅ 三段式 ✅ Yours, + Li Ming（不写真名）✅ 邮件体（不写 Subject）
