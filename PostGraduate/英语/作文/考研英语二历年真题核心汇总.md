# 考研英语二 · 历年真题作文核心汇总（2010–2025）

> **范围**：2010–2025 全国硕士研究生入学统一考试 · 英语（二）真题作文
> **来源**：希赛网 / 考研招生网 / 考研工具站 / 启航考研 / CSDN 真题自整理贴
> **用途**：按年份速查真题题型 + 核心主题词 + 可复用高分表达
> **说明**：小作文自 2010 年起为 **应用文（书信/通知，约 100 词）**；大作文自 2010 年起固定为 **图表作文（约 150 词）**

### 小作文高频核心表达（背诵清单）

**开头句（万能）**
- I am writing to **express my sincere gratitude for** …
- I am writing to **extend my invitation to you to** …
- I am writing to **make an apology to you for** …
- I am writing to **complain about** …
- I am writing to **recommend** … **to you**.

**中段句（功能句）**
- It would be of great help if you could …
- I would be much obliged / grateful if you could …
- The following suggestions might be conducive to …
- Considering the above-mentioned reasons, I sincerely hope that …

**结尾句**
- I am looking forward to your **favorable** reply at your earliest convenience.
- Your prompt attention to this matter would be highly appreciated.
- Thank you for your time and consideration.

### 大作文万能核心句型（三段式）

**第一段 · 描述图表**
- As is **vividly / clearly depicted / illustrated** in the chart above, …
- It is **noticeable / striking** that … **increased / decreased considerably from** ___ **to** ___.
- … **accounts for the lion's share, reaching** ___%, **followed by** … **at** ___%, **whereas** … **merely takes up** ___%.

**第二段 · 分析原因**
- **A number of factors could contribute to** the above-mentioned changes, **and the following are the most conspicuous ones**.
- **First and foremost**, with the rapid development of … , people tend to …
- **Furthermore**, the government has **spared no effort to promote** …
- **Last but not least**, increasing awareness of … has driven people to …

**第三段 · 评论总结**
- **From my perspective**, the trend reflected in the chart **is bound to continue** in the foreseeable future.
- It is **high time that** we **took effective measures to** …
- **Only in this way can we** achieve **sustainable development** and a **harmonious society**.

## 四、历年真题高分句（可直接套用）

1. **The bar chart above clearly reflects the changes in ___ between ___ and ___.** （2012/2014/2017/2019 通用描述句）
2. **The pie chart indicates that ___ accounts for 35%, which is almost twice as much as ___.** （2010/2013/2015 饼图对比句）
3. **This phenomenon is closely related to the rapid development of the digital economy.** （2020 后常用原因句）
4. **This trend can be mainly attributed to the policy support and rising environmental awareness.** （2021/2025 环境/新能源类通用）
5. **It is advisable for the authorities concerned to take effective measures to address this issue.** （万能建议句）
6. **Only when individuals, schools and society work together can we create a better future.** （万能结尾句）

## 五、复习建议

1. **小作文**：先按题型分类（邀请/建议/投诉/道歉/感谢/通知）各背 1 篇高分范文，再记 5-6 组"万能三段式"功能句即可覆盖 90% 考点。
2. **大作文**：必背**三段式模板各 1 套**（柱状图 / 饼图 / 表格），重点训练**数据描述段**的"精准分级副词"（considerably / slightly / steadily / sharply）。
3. **核心主题词**：对照近 5 年考点，每天背 20 个主题词，考前冲刺时按主题分组记忆（教育 / 环境 / 消费 / 文化 / 健康）。
4. **真题手写**：每周完成 1 套真题大+小作文手写，对照范文修改，积累自己的**个性化模板句**。
