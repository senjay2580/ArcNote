# OS Boot Explainer Visual Identity

## Style Prompt

Dark editorial explainer with crisp classroom clarity: deep graphite canvas, visible technical grid, warm paper panels, cyan/gold/coral accents, rectangular information cards, and smooth timeline-driven motion. The video should feel like a modern engineering lecture visualization rather than a SaaS landing page.

## Colors

- `#10141C` background graphite
- `#F7F1E8` warm paper text and panels
- `#17BEBB` cyan signal / active path
- `#F2C94C` checkpoint / memory cue
- `#FF6B4A` attention / handoff accent

## Typography

- Primary: system sans-serif with Chinese fallback
- Large headings are bold, compact, and readable at 1080p.

## What NOT To Do

- Do not use a single-color blue/purple gradient theme.
- Do not use marketing hero copy or decorative blobs.
- Do not make small low-contrast captions.
- Do not let text overlap cards or leave the 1920x1080 canvas.
